Introduction

Progressly ("the App") is a personal productivity application developed by Muhammad Shoaib Khalid ("Developer", "we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it on your Android device.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is intentionally simple because the App is designed with an offline-first, privacy-first architecture — your data belongs to you and stays with you.

Information We Do Not Collect

Progressly does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials or login information
  • Location data of any kind
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any content you enter — tasks, goals, notes, or work durations

The App has no internet connectivity. There are no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create within Progressly is stored exclusively on your device using Android's Room (SQLite) database and Jetpack DataStore. This includes:

  • Daily task entries — title, description, satisfaction rating, remarks
  • Work session durations — start time, end time, computed duration
  • Goals — title, description, priority classifications, completion status
  • App preferences — theme mode, color scheme selection
  • Onboarding completion state

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any server operated by us. Standard Android system backups (if enabled in your device settings) may include this data — this is controlled entirely by you and the Android OS, not by the App.

Permissions Used

The App requests only the following permissions, each used solely for core functionality:

POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Required on Android 13+ to display the persistent foreground service notification while a work session timer is active. No notifications are sent for marketing or tracking purposes.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE
Used to keep the work session timer running accurately when the app is backgrounded or the screen is off. The service writes session data only to the local Room database — never to any remote server.

No other permissions are requested. The App does not access contacts, camera, microphone, storage, or location.

Third-Party Services

Progressly does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect data. There are no advertisements, no analytics platforms (such as Firebase or Crashlytics), and no external dependencies that communicate over the network.

Children's Privacy

Progressly is a general-purpose productivity tool not directed at children under the age of 13. Since the App collects no personal data from any user, no special measures for children's data are applicable beyond this statement.

Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times. You can delete individual tasks, durations, or goals directly from within the App using the delete actions on each screen.

To remove all App data permanently, simply uninstall the App. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, the updated policy will be published at the same URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
