Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 20, 2026

Short version: Progressly collects zero personal data. Everything you create in the app — tasks, goals, durations — stays exclusively on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.
01

Introduction

Progressly ("the App") is a personal productivity application developed by Muhammad Shoaib Khalid ("Developer", "we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it on your Android device.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is intended to be straightforward because the App is designed with a privacy-first, offline-first architecture.

02

Information We Do Not Collect

Progressly does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account or login credentials
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any content you enter — tasks, goals, notes, or durations

The App has no internet connectivity. There are no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries included in the App.

03

Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create within Progressly is stored exclusively on your local device using Android's Room (SQLite) database and Jetpack DataStore. This includes:

  • Daily task entries (title, description, satisfaction rating, remarks)
  • Work session durations (start time, end time, computed duration)
  • Goals (title, description, priority classifications, completion status)
  • App appearance preferences (theme, color scheme)

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any server operated by us. Standard Android system backups (if enabled by you in your device settings) may include this data — this is controlled entirely by you and Android, not by the App.

04

Permissions Used

The App requests the following Android permissions, each used solely for core functionality:

POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Required on Android 13+ to display the persistent foreground service notification during an active work session timer. No notifications are sent for marketing or tracking purposes.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE
Used to run the work session timer as a foreground service, ensuring it continues accurately even when the app is backgrounded or the screen is off. The service writes session data only to the local Room database.

No other permissions are requested. The App does not request access to contacts, camera, microphone, storage, or location.

05

Third-Party Services

Progressly does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect data. There are no advertisements, no analytics platforms, and no external dependencies that communicate over the network.

06

Children's Privacy

Progressly is a general-purpose productivity tool not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the App collects no personal data from any user, no special measures for children are applicable beyond this statement.

07

Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete individual tasks, durations, or goals from within the App. To remove all App data, uninstall the App from your device — this permanently erases all locally stored data.

There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

08

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, the updated policy will be published at the same URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
